# Contribution

Contributions should keep the plugin suitable for future Homebridge Verified review.

## Guidelines

- Keep TypeScript strict and fully typed.
- Avoid unnecessary dependencies.
- Do not log API keys, tokens or private data.
- Do not add destructive SABnzbd commands unless they are explicitly requested and disabled by default.
- Update README, wiki and changelog for user-facing changes.
- Add tests for behavior changes.

## Francais

Les contributions doivent préserver la compatibilité Homebridge, la sécurité et la simplicité du plugin. Les commandes destructrices SABnzbd ne doivent pas être ajoutées sans demande explicite.
